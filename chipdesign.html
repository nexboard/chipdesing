Applied Biochip Design

Live Webinar

Tuesday, 23 June 2026

Online


Attend this webinar to gain insight into:

Automating the Microfluidic Design Process

Microfluidic system workflows and architectures integrated with design automation, spanning predictive modeling, EDA methodologies, and verification and validation.

Microfluidic Flow Simulation

CFD-based simulation and transport analysis of fluid behavior in microfluidic systems, including numerical methods and computational modeling of flow and transport.

Standardization, Modularization, and Customization

System design through reusable modules, standardized building blocks, and configurable architectures for diverse applications.

Design for Manufacturing and Assembly

Case studies, trends, and challenges in microfluidic design for manufacturing and assembly, focusing on the integration of design with advanced fabrication and assembly processes.

Date & Time Tuesday, 23 June 2026, 10:00–14:00 CEST (UTC+2)

Webinar Access:
The webinar will take place via Google Meet and can be accessed directly in a browser on desktop, tablet, or smartphone without any additional installation. A microphone and webcam may be used, but are not required. Questions may also be asked via the chat.
